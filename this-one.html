Welcome to Thunderbird Nation

When you attend Southern Utah University, it's all about you. Your Education. Your Life. Your Thunder.

We Treat You like a Person

Members of our campus community are ready to help you discover the Thunderbird within, and the experiences only we can offer. Our accomplished staff and faculty can provide you with the kind of mentorship and education that sets you up for long term success in school and in life.
